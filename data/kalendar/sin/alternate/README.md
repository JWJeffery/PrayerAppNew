# Alternate Candidate SIN Maps

These files complete the first identity-control pass for the alternate candidate rows in the first six completed Kalendar v0.1 months: July through December.

The earlier `rank1/` files control the first-ranked daily identity. These `alternate/` files control every non-rank-1 candidate row by date and rank.

## Columns

- `date`: month-day key.
- `rank`: candidate rank in the monthly candidate matrix.
- `sin`: Universal Office Saint Identification Number.
- `normalized_name`: controlled identity label.
- `entity_type`: person, group, feast, observance, commemoration, or event.
- `source_candidate_name`: the candidate label as represented in the monthly ranked matrix.
- `identity_note`: short note when the identity is a date variant, group form, source-form variant, or otherwise ambiguous.

## Completion boundary

This completes SIN assignment for the candidate rows currently present in the July-December candidate matrices. It does not mean the candidates are final selections. The monthly matrices remain pre-decisional.
