## Governance decisions preserved

1. Full matrix standard restored.

February through June are no longer treated as acceptable rank-1-only completion boundaries. They now carry alternate rows at the same structural standard as the rest of Kalendar v0.1.

2. Group source-forms remain distinct from individual persons.

Group forms such as apostolic pairs, martyr groups, biblical witness groups, and source-specific paired commemorations retain group identity treatment unless a separate individual row requires a separate SIN.

3. Similar names are not automatic identity evidence.

Name similarity remains a review trigger, not a merger rule. Identity distinctions created during the January-through-June repair remain explicit in candidate notes and SIN notes.

4. Transfer context is not source witness for the occupying fixed date.

SEC year-specific transfers remain transfer context only. They do not become fixed-date witness evidence for a different saint or observance occupying the civil date.

5. Governed exclusions remain absent.

The governed-out figure formerly present on January 15 and April 4 remains absent from the repaired matrices, alternate maps, and full audit.
